History

First made in Lei-Yang, China by Ts'ai Lun around 2,000 years ago
- Ts'ai Lun invented paper with his knowledge of science.
- He did this by mashing bark, hemp and rags with water into pulp. Than he left the pressed out liquid to dry in the sun. After the liquid dried it turned into paper.

Process

Different procces

of making paper

Most Simple (Kraft Process simplified):

Collect raw material
Collect wood chips from softwood or hardwood trees.
Pulping
Cooking: Cook wood chips in a digester with a mixture of sodium hydroxide (NaOH) and sodium sulfide (Na2S). This breaks down the lignin that binds the cellulose fibers. Liquor Separation: Separate the cooking liquor (black liquor) from the pulp.
Washing
Wash the pulp to remove chemicals and dissolved lignin.
Screening and Cleaning:
Screen and clean the washed pulp to ensure uniform consistency. Sheet Formation:
Mix the refined pulp with water and spread it onto a moving mesh screen (Fourdrinier machine) to form a continuous sheet of paper as water drains.
Pressing:
Press the wet sheet between rollers to remove excess water.
Drying:
Dry the pressed sheet using heated rollers or air dryers.
Finishing:
Smooth the dried paper with calendar rollers. Cut the paper to size and package it for distribution.
