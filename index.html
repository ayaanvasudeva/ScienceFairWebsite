History

Paper has been made for so many years. It was first made in Lei-Yang, China by Ts'ai Lun around 2,000 years ago! Ts'ai Lun invented paper with his knowledge of science. He did this by mashing bark, hemp and rags with water into pulp. Than he left the pressed out liquid to dry in the sun. After the liquid dried it turned into paper.
